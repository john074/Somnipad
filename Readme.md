**Somnipad**

A simple notepad app designed to mimic the feel of a small physical notebook.

- **UI:** Avalonia
- **Data storage:** SQLite

**Usage:**
- Click the lower part of a page to turn it.
- Press **Ctrl + O** to open the settings window.

*Early development stage: some features may be missing, bugs possible.*
